(Day 1):Mechuka is a small town (sub-divisional headquarter) located in a picturesque valley near the Indo-Tibet border. During winters, one can get great views of the snow-clad mountains and snow fall.




Mechuka- It is a natural lake situated at a high altitude with pristine and scenic surroundings. One of the oldest Buddhist monasteries of Arunachal Pradesh named Samtem Yongcha of the Mahayana sect is situated here atop a hill top overlooking the valley.
